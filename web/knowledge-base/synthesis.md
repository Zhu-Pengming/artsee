**Royal Academy of Fine Arts Antwerp** [[schools/antwerp-royal-academy/index]] ✅ VERIFIED
- One of Europe's oldest art academies (founded 1663)
- Internationally renowned for fashion design program
- Produced influential designers including the "Antwerp Six" (1980s) and Martin Margiela
- Notable historical student: Vincent van Gogh (1885-1886)
- Currently part of Artesis Plantijn Hogeschool Antwerpen (since 2013)
- **Verification status**: 4 official sources processed, major facts verified
- **Tuition verified**: €25,000/year for non-EEA students
- **Application verified**: March 25 deadline, July 1-3 in-person exam
