# Antwerp Six
# 安特卫普六君子

## Definition
"安特卫普六君子"（Antwerp Six）是指1980年代从安特卫普皇家美术学院毕业的六位时装设计师：Ann Demeulemeester、Dries Van Noten、Dirk Van Saene、Walter Van Beirendonck、Dirk Bikkembergs 和 Marina Yee。他们以独特的设计风格对当时的时尚界产生了重大影响，使安特卫普皇家美术学院的时装设计专业享誉国际。

The "Antwerp Six" refers to six fashion designers who graduated from the Royal Academy of Fine Arts Antwerp in the 1980s: Ann Demeulemeester, Dries Van Noten, Dirk Van Saene, Walter Van Beirendonck, Dirk Bikkembergs, and Marina Yee. Their distinctive design approaches had a major impact on the fashion world and brought international recognition to Antwerp's fashion program.

## Key Points
- Graduated from [[schools/antwerp-royal-academy]] in the 1980s

- Helped establish Antwerp as a major fashion education center
- Influenced subsequent generations of fashion designers
