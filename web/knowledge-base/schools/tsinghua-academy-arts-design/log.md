### Key Findings

#### Rankings & Recognition ✅
- **QS Art & Design 2025**: #14 globally, **#1 in Asia** (↑10 from 2024)
- **QS Art History 2025**: #3 globally, **#2 in Asia**
- **Ministry of Education**: Design A+, Fine Arts A-, Art Theory A-
- **Double First-Class**: All 3 disciplines (Art Theory, Fine Arts, Design)

#### Enrollment Data ✅
- **Total students**: 1,925 (1,109 undergrad, 816 grad, 127 international)
- **Faculty**: 190 teachers (72 professors, 101 associate professors)
- **Undergraduate admission**: 225-245 per year
- **Estimated acceptance rate**: ~1.3% (unverified, from third-party sources)

#### Historical Context ✅
- **Founded**: 1956 as Central Academy of Arts & Crafts
- **Merged into Tsinghua**: 1999
- **First in China**: First higher education institution for design arts
- **70th anniversary**: 2026

#### Program Structure ✅
- **10 departments**: Textile/Fashion, Ceramic, Visual Communication, Environmental, Industrial, Arts & Crafts, Information Art, Painting, Sculpture, Art History
- **20+ undergraduate programs**: Design, Fine Arts, Art History
- **3 doctoral programs**: Art Theory, Fine Arts, Design
- **3 postdoctoral stations**

#### Unique Insights from Bilibili ⚠️
- **Career path rankings**: Game design (top) > Visual/Product design > Film/Animation > Education > Architecture (worst)
- **Design industry challenges**: Long hours, moderate pay, AI disruption risk
- **Architecture crisis**: 5,000-6,000 RMB/month even at top firms, real estate contraction
- **Game industry boom**: Mihoyo, Tencent bonuses can reach 1M+ RMB annually

#### Application Requirements ✅
- **Gaokao requirement**: Special admission line + **30 points**
- **Provincial exam**: Must pass and qualify
- **School exam**: Cultural literacy test + Art ability test
- **Qualification certificates**: ≤900 issued for 225 slots
- **Tuition**: 10,000 RMB/year (domestic students)
