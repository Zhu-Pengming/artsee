## 学校概况 / Overview ✅

> ✅ **Verification Status**: VERIFIED. Key facts confirmed by encyclopedias and official data.

### Basic Information ✅
- **Founded**: 1956 as Central Academy of Arts & Crafts (中央工艺美术学院)
- **Merged into Tsinghua**: November 20, 1999
- **Historical significance**: China's first higher education institution for design arts
- **Current status**: Secondary college of Tsinghua University
- **Address**: Tsinghua University, Haidian District, Beijing 100084

### Historical Milestones
- **1956**: Central Academy of Arts & Crafts established by State Council
- **1960**: Designated as one of 12 key art academies by Ministry of Culture
- **1980**: Established Fashion Design program (first in China)
- **1986**: First institution in China to grant PhD in Arts & Crafts History & Theory
- **1999**: Merged into Tsinghua University, renamed Academy of Arts & Design
- **2004**: Ranked #1 in national first-level discipline assessment (Art & Design)
- **2021**: President Xi Jinping visited the academy (110th Tsinghua anniversary)
- **2026**: 70th anniversary of academy founding

## 申请信息 / Admissions

> ✅ **Verification Status**: VERIFIED for domestic students. International student information available.

### For Chinese Students (Gaokao + School Exam System) ✅

**Application Process** (2026):
1. **Provincial exam**: Must pass provincial art & design exam
2. **Initial screening**: Based on provincial exam scores
3. **Cultural literacy test**: Chinese, Math, English (Feb-Mar)
4. **Art ability test**: Sketching, speed drawing, color (Mar)
5. **Gaokao**: June (national college entrance exam)
6. **Admission**: Based on art ability test ranking + gaokao score

**Requirements**:
- **Gaokao score**: Must reach special admission line + **30 points** (Shanghai +26, Hainan +36)
- **Provincial exam**: Must pass and qualify
- **Health**: No color blindness
- **Qualification certificates**: ≤900 certificates issued (for 225 admission slots)

**Competition Level**:
- **Admission**: 225-245 students per year
- **Estimated applicants**: 17,500 per year (unverified)
- **Estimated acceptance rate**: ~1.3% (unverified, from third-party sources)
- **Difficulty score**: **9.5/10** (extremely competitive)

**Costs**:
- **Tuition**: 10,000 RMB/year (Design & Fine Arts)
- **Art History**: Admitted through regular gaokao (no art exam)

### Campus Atmosphere ✅
- **Comprehensive university environment**: Part of Tsinghua, not standalone art academy
- **"Art + Science" fusion**: Strong emphasis on interdisciplinary collaboration

- **Access to Tsinghua resources**: Libraries, labs, cross-disciplinary courses
- **Beijing cultural resources**: 798 Art District, museums, galleries nearby

> **Source**: [[sources/bilibili]], [[sources/manus]]

### Teaching Style ✅
- **Studio system**: Inherited from Central Academy of Arts & Crafts tradition
- **Project-based learning**: "Basic courses + studio + lab + project" model
- **Interdisciplinary emphasis**: Collaboration with Computer Science, Journalism, Engineering
- **Industry connections**: Projects with companies, government, cultural institutions

### Career Challenges (from Bilibili video) ⚠️
- **Design industry reality**: Long hours, overtime common, moderate salaries
- **AI disruption risk**: Basic design work increasingly automated
- **Limited civil service options**: Very few government positions for design majors
- **Architecture sector**: Particularly challenging job market (5,000-6,000 RMB/month even at top firms)
- **Game design**: Top career path (Tencent, Mihoyo, etc. - high salaries, bonuses can reach 1M+ RMB)

**Career Path Rankings** (from industry perspective):
1. **Game Art & Design** 🔥🔥🔥 - Top tier (Mihoyo, Tencent, Perfect World)
2. **Visual Design & Product Design** 👍👍 - Above average (ByteDance, Xiaomi, Honor)
3. **Film & Animation** 😐 - Requires passion (intense workload, AI impact)
4. **Art Education** 👌 - Decent (top performers earn 1,000+ RMB/day)
5. **Architecture** 😢 - Challenging (real estate contraction, low salaries)

## Comparison with Peer Schools

### China Tier 1 Art Academies
- **Central Academy of Fine Arts (CAFA)**: Stronger in pure fine arts (painting, sculpture, Chinese painting)
- **China Academy of Art (CAA, Hangzhou)**: Also A+ in Design, strong in humanities

### Advantages over CAFA
- Tsinghua comprehensive platform (engineering, science, business resources)
- Stronger in industrial design, information art, design management
- Better for art + technology fusion
- Higher QS international ranking

### Disadvantages vs. CAFA
- Less traditional fine arts atmosphere
- Weaker in pure painting, sculpture, traditional Chinese art
- Less focus on individual artistic expression

### Global Comparison
- **RCA (Royal College of Art)**: QS #1 vs Tsinghua #14, but RCA tuition £35,950/year vs Tsinghua 10,000 RMB/year
- **Politecnico di Milano**: Similar QS ranking (#6), European context
- **Parsons, RISD, SAIC**: Different style (more contemporary/experimental)
