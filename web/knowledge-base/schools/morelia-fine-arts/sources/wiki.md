# Universidad Michoacana de San Nicolás de Hidalgo

## TL;DR
The Universidad Michoacana de San Nicolás de Hidalgo (UMSNH) is a prestigious public university in Morelia, Michoacán, Mexico, with a rich history dating back to 1540. It is the oldest institution of higher education in the Americas and offers a wide range of degrees in various fields, including philosophy, law, economics, computer science, medicine, architecture, and dentistry. The university has a strong tradition in philosophy and research, with notable faculty and contributions to the field.

## Key Claims

### Historical Background
- **Established in 1540**: UMSNH is the oldest institution of higher education in the Americas, founded by Catholic Bishop Don Vasco de Quiroga to train priests for evangelization.
- **Royal College of Saint Nicholas**: The university started as the College of San Nicolás Obispo, receiving royal patronage in 1543.
- **National College of Saint Nicholas of Hidalgo (1847-1917)**: The college was renamed and became a national institution in 1847.
- **Reopened in 1917**: The university was reopened as the Michoacan University of San Nicolás de Hidalgo after the Mexican Revolution.

### Academic Programs
- **Wide Range of Degrees**: UMSNH offers degrees in philosophy, law, economics, computer science, medicine, architecture, dentistry, and many other fields.
- **Strong Tradition in Philosophy**: The university has a long history of teaching and research in philosophy, with influential philosophers like Samuel Ramos, María Zambrano, and Luis Villoro associated with it.
- **Faculty of Philosophy**: The Faculty of Philosophy “Dr. Samuel Ramos Magaña” focuses on advanced academic training in philosophical studies and analytic philosophy research.

### Campus and Location
- **Location**: UMSNH is located in Morelia, Michoacán, Mexico.
- **Campus**: The university has campuses in Morelia and Uruapan.

### Notable Contributions
- **Public Library**: UMSNH has a renowned public library with a rich collection of books and resources.
- **Instituto de Investigaciones Filosóficas**: The university houses the Instituto de Investigaciones Filosóficas, a leading center for analytic philosophical research in Mexico.
- **Father of the Nation Award**: The university awards the Father of the Nation Award to students with the best average.
