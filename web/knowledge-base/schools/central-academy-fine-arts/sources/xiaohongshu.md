# 中央美术学院 - Xiaohongshu UGC Collection

## TL;DR
Collection of 19 evidence-level posts from Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book) covering career outcomes, campus life, application experiences, and student perspectives. Most valuable for 2026 spring job fair data and first-hand student experiences. Evidence level: C (UGC).

## Key Claims

### Career Outcomes (2026 Spring Job Fair) 🔥

#### Job Fair Overview ✅
- **Date**: 2026 spring recruitment (央美春招双选会)
- **Companies**: 60+ companies attended
- **Major employers**: ByteDance (字节), Mihoyo (米哈游), Perfect World (完美世界), FunPlus, ANIMOON, etc.
- **Positions**: Design, concept art, exhibition planning, art education, etc.

#### Career Path Rankings (从夯到拉) ⭐⭐⭐

**1. Game Art & Design** 🔥🔥🔥 (最夯 - "God-tier")
- **Companies**: Mihoyo, Perfect World, Tencent
- **Roles**: Concept art (原画设计), game effects
- **Salary**: Top-tier, Tencent bonuses can reach 1M+ RMB annually
- **Status**: "God-tier" for art students who love games
- **Advantages**: Big company halo + benefits + year-end bonuses

**2. Visual Design & Product Design** 👍👍 (人上人 - "Above average")
- **Companies**: ByteDance, Honor (荣耀), Xiaomi, fast-moving consumer goods brands
- **Roles**: Visual designer, UI/UX designer, product designer
- **Salary**: Good annual salary
- **Status**: "Above average"

**3. Film & Animation** 😐 (需要热爱 - "Requires passion")
- **Companies**: Enlight Media (光线传媒), Fantawild (华强方特)
- **Challenges**: Intense workload, AI impact on hand-drawn industry
- **Status**: "Requires passion" - tough and competitive

**4. Art Education** 👌 (不错 - "Decent")
- **Reality**: Many graduates become art teachers
- **Income**: Top performers can earn 1,000+ RMB/day
- **Depends on**: Exam scores and background
- **Status**: "Decent"

**5. Architecture** 😢 (拉完了 - "Terrible")
- **Salary**: 5,000-6,000 RMB/month even at top firms
- **Industry**: Real estate contraction
- **Status**: "Terrible" - many want to switch careers
- **Note**: Consistent with Bilibili video observation about architecture

#### Industry Trends 💡
- Art students increasingly going into: **Content / Games / Branding / Commercial** sectors
- Not just "pure art industry" anymore
- **Design students most popular** at job fairs
- Comprehensive skills (综合能力) increasingly important

### Application Experience (港澳台学生) ⭐

#### Hong Kong/Macau/Taiwan Students (港澳台美术生)
- **Policy**: "文过专排" (Culture pass, major rank)
  - Lower cultural score requirement
  - Ranked by professional exam score
- **Training time**: 0 foundation to passing - 2 months sufficient
  - Example: Guangzhou Academy exam - 2 months total training, ranked 32
  - CAFA: Less than 1 month training, passed qualification line
- **Exam content**: Different from mainland students
- **Success rate**: "Most Hong Kong/Macau/Taiwan classmates passed steadily"

#### Critical: Studio Selection ⚠️
- **Not all studios understand HK/Macau/Taiwan policy**
- Different admission rules, exam rules, application timeline
- Different exam content - need separate training
- Portfolio submission process different
- **Must ask**: Studio's HK/Macau/Taiwan arrangements and dedicated staff

### Course Examples 📖

#### Sculpture Department: "Space Analysis" (空间解析)
- **Requirements**:
  1. Find something you're interested in (good at)
  2. Find a space on campus
  3. Start from space's limitations
  4. Consider time and people
  5. Record work-audience interaction, spread via internet
  6. Give part of judgment authority to audience

#### Design School: Industrial Design Course (2025)
- **Course**: 产业设计, 设计学概论, 社会创新与可持续未来设计
- **Professor**: Zhang Ming (张明教授, 2025.11.28 sharing)

#### Design Course Learning Approach
- **Build "problem awareness"** and knowledge system
- Cross-disciplinary knowledge connections
- Thinking training and tool usage
- Emphasis on conceptual thinking over just drawing skills
