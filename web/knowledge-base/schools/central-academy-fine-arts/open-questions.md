## Unverified Claims (需要核实)

### 1. "Best Art Academy in China" 🤔
**Claim**: CAFA is the #1 art academy in China

**Evidence FOR**:
- ✅ QS Art & Design #14 (2026), highest among Chinese art academies
- ✅ Ministry of Education: Fine Arts A+, Design A+
- ✅ Only art academy with TWO "Double First-Class" disciplines
- ✅ Historical prestige (founded 1918, first president Xu Beihong)

**Evidence AGAINST**:
- ⚠️ China Academy of Art (中国美院) also has A+ in Fine Arts
- ⚠️ Tsinghua Academy of Art & Design has A+ in Design
- ⚠️ "Best" is subjective - depends on program and criteria

**Verdict**: **PARTIALLY VERIFIED** - Top tier, but "best" depends on program
- Fine Arts (Chinese painting, oil painting): Likely #1
- Design: Tied with Tsinghua and China Academy of Art
- Overall prestige: Top 3 in China
