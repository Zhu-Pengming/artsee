**Claim**: Overall acceptance rate is approximately 10%

**Evidence FOR**:
- ✅ PSL University page states this figure
- ✅ 70 first-year slots + 25 transfer slots = 95 total
- ⚠️ Estimated applicants: ~950 per year (if 10% is accurate)

**Evidence AGAINST**:
- ❌ Some third-party sources claim 21% or 67.6%
- ❌ No official applicant numbers published
- ❌ Numbers may vary by year and entry level

**Verdict**: **LIKELY ACCURATE** - PSL is reliable source, but verify with official data
- Use as general competitiveness indicator
- Do not cite as exact acceptance rate without official confirmation
