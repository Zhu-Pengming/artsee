### Key Findings

#### Rankings & Recognition ✅
- **QS Art & Design 2026**: #40 globally
- **"World's Four Great Art Academies"**: Alongside Florence, Bologna, Repin
- **"Musée de France" status** (2017): Second-largest art collection in France after Louvre
- **PSL University member**: Cross-disciplinary resources

#### Historical Context ✅
- **Founded**: 1648 as Royal Academy of Painting and Sculpture
- **Current name**: 1817 (École nationale supérieure des Beaux-Arts)
- **375+ years of history**: One of world's oldest art academies
- **Collection**: 450,000+ artworks

#### Enrollment Data ✅
- **Total students**: ~550
- **International students**: ~20% (~110 students)
- **Faculty**: ~80 teachers
- **First-year admission**: ~70 students per year
- **Acceptance rate**: ~10% (estimated, from PSL data)

#### Unique Atelier System ✅
- **NOT traditional major divisions**: No "Painting Department" or "Sculpture Department"
- **40-50 artist workshops**: Students choose renowned artist's studio
- **Mentor selection**: Mutual selection between teachers and students
- **Cross-media practice**: Not limited to single medium
- **Evaluation**: Portfolio reviews, exhibitions, not traditional exams

#### Application Requirements ✅
- **Age limits**: 18-24 for first year, under 26 for transfer
- **French language**: **B2 minimum** (strongly recommended for first year, required for transfer)
- **Portfolio**: PDF (max 10-20MB) + optional videos
- **Entrance exam**: Theory test + drawing test + 15-min oral interview
- **Application fee**: 56 EUR

#### Tuition & Costs ✅
- **French/EU students**: ~438 EUR/year (symbolic fee)
- **Non-EU international students**: ~13,100 EUR/year
- **Living costs**: 600-1,200 EUR/month (Paris center), 300-600 EUR/month (suburbs)
- **CAF subsidy**: 150-200 EUR/month (housing assistance)

#### Notable Alumni ✅
- **Chinese pioneers**: Xu Beihong, Lin Fengmian, Wu Guanzhong, Pan Yuliang
- **Western masters**: Matisse, Degas, Monet, Renoir, Braque
- **Fashion**: Givenchy, Valentino
