## Key Claims

### School Overview
- **Established in 1896** by William Merritt Chase, Parsons is a prestigious design school located in New York City.
- **Part of The New School** and recognized as the only independent art and design college within a private research university.
- Offers over 30 degree programs ranging from AAS to PhD, with a focus on fashion design, fine arts, and design strategies.
- Has a student body of approximately 5,500, with international students accounting for about 31%.

### Program-Level Data
- **BFA Fashion Design** is the flagship program with a reputation as the top fashion design school in the US.
- **BFA Communication Design** offers a comprehensive program in graphic design, brand, and information design.
- **BFA Design and Technology** focuses on UX/UI, interaction, new media art, and generative AI.
- **BFA Fine Arts** is a smaller program within the School of Art, Media and Technology (AMT).
- **BBA Strategic Design and Management** is a unique business and design thinking program.

### Admission Requirements
- **Language Requirements**: TOEFL iBT min 92, IELTS min 7.0, Duolingo min 115.
- **Portfolio Requirements**: Varies by program, with a focus on creative expression and conceptual thinking.
- **Application Process**: Common App for undergraduates and New School Online Application for graduates.

### Tuition and Funding
- **Tuition**: Varies by program and level of study, with estimates provided for each.
- **Financial Aid**: Offers merit scholarships and need-based aid for undergraduate students. Graduate funding includes teaching assistantships, research fellowships, and student employment opportunities.

### Campus Facilities and Resources
- Offers a range of facilities including classrooms, libraries, studios, and workshops.
- Provides access to The New School's library system, archives, and cross-disciplinary courses.
- Has strong connections with New York's art scene, including MoMA, the Met, and the Whitney Museum.

### Student Life and Experience
- Offers a vibrant campus life with a diverse student body and a range of student organizations and events.
- Provides support for international students, including ESL courses and cultural programming.
- Offers opportunities for internships and industry connections through its strong alumni network.

### Career Outcomes
- Claims a high employment rate for graduates, with many entering the fashion, media, and technology industries.
- Alumni include notable figures such as Tom Ford, Donna Karan, and Marc Jacobs.
