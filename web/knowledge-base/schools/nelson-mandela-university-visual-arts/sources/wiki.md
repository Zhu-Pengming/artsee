# Nelson Mandela University - Wikipedia

## TL;DR
Nelson Mandela University, originally established as Port Elizabeth Art School in 1882, is a public university in Gqeberha, South Africa. The university has a rich history, having merged with several institutions, including the University of Port Elizabeth and Vista University, to become a comprehensive institution offering a wide range of professional and vocational training programs. The university is known for its diverse student body and its commitment to community engagement and social change.

## Key Claims
### History and Formation
- Nelson Mandela University was established in 1882 as Port Elizabeth Art School, making it one of the oldest art schools in South Africa.
- The university underwent several name changes and mergers, including becoming Nelson Mandela Metropolitan University in 2005 and Nelson Mandela University in 2017.
- The university was formed through a merger of three institutions: the University of Port Elizabeth, Port Elizabeth Technikon, and Vista University.
- The merger was part of a larger plan to restructure higher education in South Africa, initiated in 2002.

### Campuses and Location
- The university has seven campuses, six in Gqeberha and one in George, South Africa.
- The main campus, South Campus, is located in Summerstrand, Gqeberha, and is situated on a nature reserve, making it unique in South Africa.
- The university's campuses offer a range of facilities, including residential areas, sports complexes, and academic buildings.

### Programs and Courses
- Nelson Mandela University offers a wide range of programs, from diplomas to doctoral level degrees.
- Many courses include workplace experience as part of the curriculum, providing students with practical skills.
- The university is known for its professional and vocational training programs.

### Governance and Administration
- The university is governed by a council, with the chancellor and vice-chancellor playing key roles.
- The current chancellor is Naledi Pandor, and the vice-chancellor is Sibongile Muthwa.
- The university has an executive dean for each faculty, as well as other management committee positions.

### Student Enrollment and Diversity
- In 2020, the university had approximately 28,000 students enrolled, with a diverse student body representing various ethnic backgrounds.
- The university has a history of welcoming students from socially and financially disadvantaged backgrounds, as seen with Vista University's focus on accessible and affordable higher education.

### Notable Alumni and Staff
- The university has produced notable alumni in various fields, including science, sports, literature, and business.
- Notable alumni include Nashwa Eassa, a nano-particle physicist; Dave Richardson, ICC general manager and former Proteas cricket player; and Laduma Ngxokolo, fashion designer and businessman.
