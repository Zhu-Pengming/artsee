### Who Qualifies as "Foreign Student"?

**Foreign student tuition DOES NOT apply to**:

1. ✅ Nationals of European Economic Area (EEA) member states
2. ✅ Students with permanent residence in Belgium
3. ✅ Victims of human trafficking (certified)
4. ✅ Students with temporary residence (Article 48/4)
5. ✅ Students legally residing in Belgium for 12+ months (not for study/work purposes)
6. ✅ Family members of EEA nationals
7. ✅ Students with temporary protection (Directive 2001/55/EC)
8. ✅ Candidate refugees (specific conditions)
9. ✅ Students in Dutch-taught programs NOT at School of Arts

> **Source**: Page 2-3, Section V exceptions

**CRITICAL**: Chinese students typically qualify as "foreign students" and pay €25,000 for 60 credits.

### Fashion Show Participation Fee

**Visual Arts, main subject Fashion** (Bachelor and Master):
- **Fee**: €300
- **Purpose**: Participation in the Show
- **Refund**: Only if student is NOT allowed to show their collection (decided after mid-year jury in January)

> **Source**: Page 6, Section 1.4.3 "Costs Show"

---

### Entrance Exam Administrative Fee

**Royal Academy of Fine Arts Antwerp**:
- **One discipline**: €30
- **Additional disciplines**: +€15 each
- **Master admissions** (transferring students): €30
- **Never refunded**

> **Source**: Page 6, Section 1.4.4 "Artistic admissions test"
