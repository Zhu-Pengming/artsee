## VERIFIED FACTS: Language Requirements

### English-Language Program

**Level required**: B2 (CEFR - Common European Framework of Reference)

**You have sufficient English if you have**:

1. ✅ ASO diploma or secondary education diploma (Flanders, level 4, transfer finality)
2. ✅ VWO diploma (Netherlands)
3. ✅ Diploma from English-language secondary education
4. ✅ Diploma from English-language higher education
5. ✅ **OR** one of the following language certificates (from past 2 years only):

| Certificate | Minimum Score | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **IELTS Academic** | 6.5 overall, 6.0 each section | ⚠️ Test centre only, online NOT accepted |
| **TOEFL iBT** | 80 (exams up to Jan 20, 2026)<br>4.5 (exams from Jan 21, 2026) | ⚠️ Test centre only, home edition NOT accepted |
| **Cambridge English** | B2 First | — |
| **Pearson PTE Academic** | 59 | — |
| **ITACE** | B2 | — |

> **Source**: Official registration page, "Language requirements" section

**CRITICAL**: 
- Only certificates from **past 2 years** accepted
- Online IELTS/TOEFL **NOT accepted**
- Language certificate is **mandatory requirement for enrollment**

## VERIFIED FACTS: Diploma Requirements

### General Requirements

**Belgium**: Diploma of secondary education or equivalent

**Netherlands**: 
- ✅ VWO diploma
- ✅ HBO propedeuse
- ❌ HAVO diploma does NOT give access
- ❌ MBO diploma does NOT give access

**Other countries**: Diploma that gives access to university studies in country of origin

> **Source**: Official registration page, "Diploma requirements" section

### Chinese Students (CRITICAL)

**APS Screening Required**:
- Must complete APS screening in Beijing
- Purpose: Prevent document fraud, ensure language knowledge
- Required for student visa from Belgian embassy/consulate in China
- Contact: info@aps.org.cn
- Website: www.aps.org.cn

> **Source**: Official registration page, "Do you have a Chinese diploma?" section

## VERIFIED FACTS: Entrance Exam Structure

### Pre-Selection (Digital Portfolio)

**All disciplines including Fashion**: Digital portfolio submission

**Portfolio upload**:
- Link sent from January 9, 2026
- Deadline: April 1, 2026, 14:00 (Fashion)
- Jury decides based on portfolio whether you can participate in July exam

> **Source**: Official registration page, section 3 "Portfolio"

---

### Artistic Entrance Exam (In-Person)

**Fashion**: July 1-3, 2026 (3 days)
- **Day 1-2**: Artistic assignments in studio with tutors
- **Day 3**: Drawing assignments on main campus
- **Format**: In-person ONLY, no digital option

**Assessment criteria**:
- Artistic process
- Creativity and imagination
- Interest in general culture, art/design, visual culture
- Drawing skills
- Ability to express ideas in image and language

> **Source**: Official registration page, sections 5 "Artistic assignments"

**CRITICAL**: Fashion candidates CANNOT take digital exam. Must attend physically.
