### Language Requirements ✅

**Level required**: B2 (CEFR - Common European Framework of Reference)

**Accepted certificates** (must be from past 2 years only):
- **IELTS Academic**: 6.5 overall, 6.0 minimum each section (test centre only, online NOT accepted)
- **TOEFL iBT**: 80 (test centre only, home edition NOT accepted)
- **Cambridge English**: B2 First
- **Pearson PTE Academic**: 59
- **ITACE**: B2

**OR** diploma from English-language secondary/higher education

> **Source**: [[official/bachelor-registration-2026-27]]
> **CRITICAL**: Only test centre exams accepted. Online IELTS/TOEFL NOT accepted.

### Chinese Students ✅

**APS Certificate REQUIRED** for Chinese diploma holders:
- Must complete APS screening in Beijing
- Purpose: Prevent document fraud, ensure language knowledge
- Required for student visa from Belgian embassy/consulate
- Contact: info@aps.org.cn

> **Source**: [[official/bachelor-registration-2026-27]]

### Entrance Exam Structure ✅

**Pre-selection**: Digital portfolio submission (deadline Apr 1, 2026)

**Artistic Entrance Exam** (Jul 1-3, 2026):
- **Day 1-2**: Artistic assignments in studio with tutors
- **Day 3**: Drawing assignments on main campus
- **Format**: In-person ONLY for Fashion (no digital option)

**Assessment criteria**:
- Artistic process
- Creativity and imagination
- Interest in general culture, art/design, visual culture
- Drawing skills
- Ability to express ideas in image and language

> **Source**: [[official/bachelor-registration-2026-27]]

#### Tuition Fees 2026-27 (Fashion Bachelor/Master, 60 credits) ✅

**EEA Students** (non-scholarship):
- Fixed: €305.40
- Per credit: €14.60
- **60 credits total**: **€1,181.40**
- Course-related fee: €185
- Fashion show fee: €300
- **TOTAL**: **€1,666.40**

**Non-EEA Students** (including Chinese students):
- Fixed: €2,500.00
- Per credit: €375.00
- **60 credits total**: **€25,000.00**
- Course-related fee: €185
- Fashion show fee: €300
- **TOTAL**: **€25,485.00**

**Entrance Exam Fee**:
- One discipline: €30 (non-refundable)
- Two disciplines: €45 (non-refundable)

> **Source**: [[official/tuition-2026-27]]
> **CRITICAL**: Non-EEA students pay **15.3× more** than EEA students.
> **Note**: Fashion show fee (€300) only refunded if student NOT allowed to show collection (decided after mid-year jury in January).
