# Official Information from the Royal Academy of Fine Arts Antwerp

## TL;DR
The Royal Academy of Fine Arts Antwerp offers a range of visual arts programs at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. The institution is known for its vibrant artistic community and strong ties to the local and international art scenes. The Academy has recently announced an increase in tuition fees for non-EEA students, which has been met with sympathy from the staff. The Academy is committed to fostering artistic talent and offers various events, exhibitions, and initiatives to support its students and alumni.

## Key Claims
### Programs and Education
- The Academy offers Bachelor and Master degrees in Visual Arts.
- Programs include Sculpture, Photography, Graphic Design, Fine Arts - In Situ, Jewellery design & gold- and silversmithing, Costume Design, Fashion, Fine Arts - Painting, Fine Arts - Printmaking and Drawing, and an Educational master in Visual Arts.
- The Academy emphasizes experimentation, critical thinking, and individual approaches to artistic practice.

### Events and Initiatives
- The Academy hosts the Antwerp (Academy) Art Weekend and SHOW + EXPO events.
- The Academy is involved in cultural work, exhibitions, and research projects.
- The Academy has launched an alumni network, the Antwerp Academy Ambassadors.

### Tuition and Fees
- There has been an increase in tuition fees for non-EEA students.
- The staff at the Academy deeply sympathizes with the students affected by the fee increase.

### Campus and Location
- The Academy is located at Mutsaardstraat 31, 2000 Antwerpen, Belgium.
- The Academy has a strong presence in the local art scene and is part of the broader cultural landscape of Antwerp.
