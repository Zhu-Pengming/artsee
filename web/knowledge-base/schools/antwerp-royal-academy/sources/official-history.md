# Royal Academy of Fine Arts Antwerp - Official Description

## TL;DR
The Royal Academy of Fine Arts Antwerp, founded in 1663, is a prestigious international art school located in the heart of Antwerp, Belgium. It boasts a diverse student body of over 650 students from 50 different nationalities and offers a range of programs in various art disciplines. The Academy is renowned for its rich history, influential alumni, and commitment to artistic research and cultural engagement.

## Key Claims
### History and Establishment
- **Established in 1663**: The Academy is one of the oldest art schools in the world, founded by artist David Teniers the Younger.
- **Historic Campus**: The school's location in Antwerp's city centre and its beautiful architecture reflect its long-standing presence and cultural significance.

### Student Body
- **650 Students**: The Academy hosts a diverse community of students, with over 50 different nationalities represented.
- **International Reputation**: The school's global appeal is evident in its diverse student body and international faculty.

### Programs Offered
- **Wide Range of Programs**: The Academy offers programs in Costume Design, Fashion, Graphic Design, In Situ³, Jewellery Design, Painting, Photography, Printmaking, Sculpture, and an Educational Master.
- **Educational Master**: The Master's program allows students to transition into visual arts teacher training, emphasizing the Academy's commitment to education and professional development.

### Campus and Facilities
- **Beautiful Architecture**: The Academy's campus, designed by Pierre Bourla and others, is a testament to its rich history and architectural heritage.
- **Historic Garden**: The protected garden, reactivated as an exhibition space in 2021, adds to the cultural vibrancy of the campus.

### Notable Alumni
- **Influential Alumni**: The Academy has produced renowned alumni such as Henry Van De Velde, Panamarenko, Anne-Mie Van Kerckhoven, the Antwerp Six, and Demna Gvasalia, highlighting its contribution to the art world.
- **Vincent Van Gogh's Presence**: The school's association with Vincent Van Gogh, who spent a short time there, adds to its historical significance.

### Commitment to Artistic Research and Culture
- **High-Level Artistic Research**: The Academy is committed to pushing the boundaries of artistic expression through research and cultural programming.
- **Cultural Calendar**: The Academy's active cultural calendar reflects its role as a hub for artistic thought and creation.
