## Useful Chinese Context

### Linda Loppa's Role (琳达·洛帕)
- Graduated 1971 from fashion program
- Opened boutique "Louis" selling avant-garde fashion
- First to introduce Yohji Yamamoto, Comme des Garçons to Belgium
- Returned to teach in 1981
- Helped "Antwerp Six" debut at London Fashion Week 1987
- Led fashion program for 20+ years
- Mentored Veronique Branquinho, Raf Simons, Kris Van Assche, An Vandevorst, Bernard Willhelm, Haider Ackermann
