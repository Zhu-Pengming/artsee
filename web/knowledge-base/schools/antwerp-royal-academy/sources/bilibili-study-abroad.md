- **Famous alumni**: Antwerp Six, Vincent van Gogh, Henry van de Velde, Raf Simons, Chris Van Assche

- **Overall coverage**: Fine Arts, Architecture, Design

- **8 total specializations** mentioned: Photography, Graphic Design, Jewelry Design, Fashion Design, etc.

### Fashion Design Program (Student-Reported)
> ⚠️ Subjective assessment, need verification

- **Reputation**: "Industry leader" due to Antwerp Six influence (1980s)
- **Philosophy**: Focuses on creativity and individual designer strength, NOT commercial/marketing focus
- **Workload** (claimed):
  - Weekly: 10+ fashion illustrations (A2/A3 size) + 10+ project assignments
  - Year 2-3: All work must use white muslin fabric (白坯布), students DIY everything (pattern, details, fabric, dyeing)
  - Year 3-4: Taught by 2 members of Antwerp Six personally
- **Requirements**: Must master 2D to 3D conversion, atmosphere drawing (氛围研发) for all projects
- **High intensity**: High dropout rate, strict professor standards

### Photography Program (Student-Reported)
> ⚠️ Need verification

- **Facilities**: 2 printing rooms (1 darkroom for film/B&W, 1 digital room for scanning/printing)
- **Resources**: Students can connect with many designers and models (advantage for fashion photography)
- **Exhibition opportunities**: Multiple independent galleries friendly to young artists

### Jewelry Design Program (Student-Reported)
> ⚠️ Need verification

- **Portfolio requirements**: Extremely high, must show craftsmanship, thinking ability, hands-on skills
- **Core courses**: "Concept and Design" + "Jewelry Design Technical Skills" = 45% of annual credits
- **Facilities**: Multiple studios for design, experimentation, discussion, manufacturing, exhibition
- **Master students**: Have dedicated personal workspace

### Application Requirements
> ⚠️ **CRITICAL - NEED OFFICIAL VERIFICATION**

**Materials**:
- Higher education degree certificate
- Application certificate
- Portfolio

**Language Requirements** (3 options for English-taught programs):
1. ASO degree or BWO diploma, OR proof of at least 1 year English-taught secondary education
2. Proof of completing at least 60 ECTS credits in English-taught higher education
3. CEFR B2 language certificate (interview also tests language ability)

**Entrance Exam** (claimed):
- **Duration**: 2 days including interview
- **Materials to bring**: CV, portfolio, drawing materials for specific major
- **Fashion Design exam**: Bring A2/A4 watercolor paper, sketch paper; complete on-site design drawing and presentation
- **Portfolio requirements** (Fashion): Sketches, observational drawings, creative graphic works, personal fashion design photos; NO need for finished garments or digital presentations

### Costs
> ⚠️ **CRITICAL - NEED OFFICIAL VERIFICATION**

**Tuition** (claimed, from "school official website"):
- **Base tuition**: €948.2 per semester
- **Per credit**: Additional €104.2 per credit
- **Fashion program extra**: €200 for annual June fashion show

**Living Costs** (claimed):
- **Housing**: ~€500/month
- **Food**: ~€200/month
- **Total**: Can control under €1,000/month if not frequently shopping/traveling
- **Comparison**: Much lower than Paris, London

**Note**: Shopping hours limited - stores close at 6pm Monday-Saturday

### Life in Antwerp (Student-Reported)
> ⚠️ Subjective observations

- **City size**: Small
- **Art resources**: Many museums and galleries; galleries often email students about exhibitions
- **Lifestyle**: Slow-paced city
- **Fashion culture**: Fashion integrated into daily life across all age groups
- **Vintage shopping**: Many vintage shops and independent designer stores
- **Landmarks**: Gothic church towers, Chinatown arch
