# Alberta University of the Arts - Wikipedia

## TL;DR
The Alberta University of the Arts (AUArts) is a public art university in Calgary, Canada, with a rich history dating back to 1926. It offers undergraduate and graduate programs in art, craft, and design through four schools. The university has a diverse student body, a strong emphasis on Indigenous culture, and a vibrant campus with libraries, galleries, and student-run spaces.

## Key Claims

### History and Establishment
- **Established in 1926**: The university's roots trace back to the art department of the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT), which was founded in 1926.
- **Evolution**: The institution has undergone several name changes, including the Alberta College of Art (ACA) and the Alberta College of Art and Design (ACAD), before becoming the Alberta University of the Arts in 2019.
- **Public Institution**: AUArts is publicly funded and operates as a co-educational institution.

### Campus and Facilities
- **Urban Campus**: Located in Calgary, AUArts has an urban campus with access to the city's cultural scene.
- **Library and Galleries**: The university boasts the Luke Lindoe Library with over 25,000 art and design-related titles, and two professional galleries, the Illingworth Kerr Gallery and the Marion Nicoll Gallery.
- **Indigenous Resource Centre**: The Lodgepole Center is an all-inclusive space that supports elder advising, traditional ceremonies, and workshops.

### Academic Programs
- **Degree Programs**: AUArts offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in art, craft, and design.
- **Schools**: The university is organized into four schools: Craft + Emerging Media, Visual Arts, Communication Design, and Critical + Creative Studies.
- **Specializations**: Programs cover a range of disciplines, including ceramics, fibre, glass, jewellery + metals, media arts, drawing, painting, print media, photography, sculpture, and more.

### Student Body and Community
- **Student Count**: As of 2023-24, there are 1,015 full-time equivalent students.
- **Indigenous Focus**: The university has a strong emphasis on Indigenous culture and offers programs and resources to support Indigenous students and communities.
- **International Students**: The student body is diverse, with a significant international presence.
